About Hotel United

Hotel United Limited, a pioneer in Nepal's hospitality sector, is located in Lalbandi and offers a deluxe hotel experience, renowned for its rich heritage and legendary hospitality serving customers for over a decade.

In addition to premium accommodations, the hotel provides exceptional restaurant services for events, a full-service bar, a state-of-the-art gym, a relaxing pool house, and a swimming pool, ensuring guests have a memorable stay.
